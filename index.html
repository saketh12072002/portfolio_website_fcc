About Us

Civil Engineering Association (CEA) is a student run organization of Department of Civil Engineering IIT MADRAS. We organize CEA Fest each year with the primary motive of bringing together avid civil engineering students across the country and providing them a chance to explore the field. With more than 1500 registrations each year, we are one of the best technical fests for Civil Engineering Students

Ever wanted to be a part of this deemed technical fest?

We bring to you CEA Campus Ambassador Program.Through this program,you get to represent CEA in your college during your tenure as Campus Ambassador.You get to work along side the organizing team, jointly organize worshops/events in your college and lot more.That being said, we also have rewards in store for you. So why miss this opportunity, register here.

Responsibilities

Publicize

Helping us reach out to the students of your college
1)Putting up posters
2)Forwarding mails to your college domain

Social Media

Promoting the events of CEA fest through social platforms like facebook, instagram, twitter etc,.

Promote

Ensure enthusiastic participation from the students of your college in events organized by CEA

Terms & Conditions

  • One or more Campus Ambassadors will be selected based on the strength of the college
  • The tasks for the Campus Ambassadors shall be conveyed through CA website and Whatsapp groups
  • Proofs of the tasks must be uploaded in the CA website
  • The Certificate of Appreciation shall be provided only upon the successful completion of the complete term
  • The representation can be revoked at any time of the year if the CA fails to perform reasonably well
  • Rules for allocation of points and other miscellaneous rules shall be elaborated in due course
